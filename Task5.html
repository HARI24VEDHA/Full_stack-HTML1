TOP 5 FAVOURITE MOVIES:

    INTERSTELLAR
  • YEAR: 2014
  • ACTORS: MATTHEW MCCONAUGHEY, ANNE HATHAWAY, JESSICA CHASTAIN
  • BRIEF DESCRIPTION: "Interstellar" is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story is set in a dystopian future where Earth is suffering from severe ecological disasters. A team of explorers, led by former NASA pilot Cooper (played by Matthew McConaughey), is sent on a mission through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new habitable planet for humanity. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the survival of the human race, with stunning visual effects and a thought-provoking narrative about the mysteries of space and time.
    SPIDER-MAN: ACCROSS THE SPIDER VERSE
  • YEAR: 2022
  • ACTORS: SHAMEIK MOORE, HAILEE STEINFELD, BRIAN TYREE HENRY
  • BRIEF DESCRIPTION: "Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse" is an animated superhero film that continues the story of Miles Morales, the teenage Spider-Man from Brooklyn. In this sequel, Miles embarks on an epic adventure across the multiverse, where he encounters different versions of Spider-Man from various dimensions. Teaming up with familiar friends like Gwen Stacy (Spider-Woman) and meeting new Spider-heroes, Miles faces formidable new enemies that threaten the fabric of the multiverse. The film explores themes of identity, responsibility, and heroism, all while delivering stunning animation and a gripping, action-packed narrative.
    DOCTOR STRANGE IN THE MULTIVERSE OF MADNESS
  • YEAR: 2022
  • ACTORS: BENEDICT CUMBERBATCH, ELIZABETH OLSEN, CHIWETEL EJIOFOR, BENEDICT WONG, RACHEL MCADAMS, XOCHITL GOMEZ, MICHAEL STUHLBARG
  • BRIEF DESCRIPTION: In "Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness," Dr. Stephen Strange (played by Benedict Cumberbatch) delves into the mysteries of the multiverse. With the help of mystical allies old and new, including Wanda Maximoff (Scarlet Witch, played by Elizabeth Olsen), Strange embarks on a mind-bending journey across different realities. As he faces new and powerful adversaries, Strange must confront his own limits and the consequences of his actions, all while trying to prevent a catastrophic collapse of the multiverse. The film combines elements of horror and fantasy, delivering thrilling action sequences and exploring deeper aspects of the MCU's mystical lore.
    LOKI
  • YEAR: 2021
  • ACTORS: TOM HIDDLESTON, OWEN WILSON, GUGU MBATHA-RAW, WUNMI MOSAKU, SOPHIA DI MARTINO, RICHARD E. GRANT, JONATHAN MAJORS
  • BRIEF DESCRIPTION: "Loki" follows the character Loki, played by Tom Hiddleston, after he escapes with the Tesseract during the events of "Avengers: Endgame." The series explores his journey as he encounters the Time Variance Authority (TVA), an organization that manages the timeline of the multiverse. As Loki grapples with his own identity and destiny, he discovers the existence of alternate versions of himself and faces various challenges that threaten the stability of the multiverse. The show blends elements of science fiction, fantasy, and character-driven drama, delving into themes of identity, purpose, and the nature of free will.
